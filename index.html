Hi Nicole

This page was made just for you.

If you're reading this, I hope you remember that you don't have to have everything figured out today. Life can be overwhelming sometimes, and I know there are days when everything feels heavier than usual. There may be moments when you feel lost, tired, or unsure of what to do next. There may even be times when you blame yourself for things that aren't entirely your fault or feel like you're falling behind while everyone else seems to be moving forward. But please be kind to yourself. You're carrying more than most people can see, and the fact that you're still trying every day says a lot about your strength. Even if your progress feels slow, you're still moving forward. Even if today wasn't perfect, you still made it through. And sometimes, that's already something to be proud of. Please don't pressure yourself to have all the answers right now. Not everything needs to be solved today. Some things take time, and that's okay. Growth isn't always noticeable, and healing isn't always fast. Be patient with yourself the same way you would be patient with someone you care about. Take care of yourself, even in the smallest ways. Drink some water. Get enough rest when you can. Eat something you enjoy. Listen to your favorite songs. Watch something that makes you laugh. Those little moments of care may seem small, but they matter more than you realize. And if today feels difficult, just focus on getting through today. You don't need to worry about tomorrow yet. One step at a time is enough. You don't have to be strong every second of every day. You're allowed to have bad days. You're allowed to feel tired. You're allowed to rest. Your worth is not measured by your grades, achievements, productivity, or how much you do for other people. You are valuable simply because you're you. Even on your worst days, you are still deserving of kindness, patience, and love. Things may not be perfect right now, but they won't stay difficult forever. So please keep going. Take your time. Be gentle with yourself. Better days are coming, even if you can't see them yet. And if nobody has told you this today, let me say it: I'm proud of you. Proud of you for trying. Proud of you for holding on. Proud of you for continuing to move forward despite everything. Keep going, okay? You've got this. ♡ — Someone who believes in you 🩵
